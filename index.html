Some common questions
were often asked

How can I look up my order?

You can look up your order by loging in and clicking the basket. You can see the purchased history in your account.

How can I place my order?

You can place your order by clicking the add to cart button on the item that you want and then proceding to your cart to check it out.

Where can I see the products?

You can see our prodects on the "products" section. You can also try to filter it out to see what you are looking for.

How can I contact the seller?

You can contact us through the email and contact number provided on the website. You can also contact us using the form on the "Contact Us" page. Thank you.

Do you ship internationally?

Sadly, our store on accept shipping withing the country Philippnes. You can still subscribe to our newsletter and we will inform you when international shipping is available.

How long does it takes for the goods to be delivered?

The arrival of goods depends on the distance of the receiver. It can also be affected by various factors such as quarantine restrictions, natural disasters and others. Rest assured that we will inform you if there are changes in the estimated arrival of the product.

What are the mode of payments available?

We accept e-payments such as gcash, bank, and cash on delivery.

Can I cancel/change order?

Yes, you can cancel or change your order as long as we haven't ship it out yet. But if the item was already shipped out, you cannot cancel your order.

What is your return policy?

You can return the item if it arrived damaged. You can only return the item if it still within the return of 7 days. Please provide proof that the item was damaged when it arrived.

How to return an item?

You can return the item by contacting us. Please provide the proof of payment, proof (picture or video) of the damaged item, and your contact information.
